##Companies act 2006
* As of 2008, a company is small if it has:
	* A turnover of not more than £6.5m
	* A balance sheet of not more than £3.26
	* Not more than 50 employees

##European commission definition
* Max number of employees:
	* Micro: 9
	* Small: 50
	* Medium: 250
* Annual turnover
	* Micro: 2m
	* Small: 10m
	* Medium: 50m
